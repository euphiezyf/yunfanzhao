Compass

Members: Jinghan Cao, Danlin Jiang
Duration: January - March, 2021, 10 weeks

Background

What was our original intention when we wrote our first diary? Why do we keep a diary and why do most of us have failed to handwrite diaries? To answer these questions and help people track and reflect on their daily life in this digital era, we develop the Compass as a simple and secured mobile web app for capturing life moments.

Problem Statement

The physical diary is limited in pages and hard to carry around, but the memory was, is, and will be infinite for our whole life. Besides, people are easy to forget about past moments in their lives. Consequently, they will lose the happiness of reviewing and reflecting.

Potential Solution

Users can capture their life moments as soon as events happen. They do not need to wait until they get home and pull out their physical diary and write things down based on their memory which could possibly lose detail.

Design Challenge

According to our user testing based on the paper prototype, we found some problems related to the heuristics and some practical issues.

#1 User Control and Freedom

We failed to make the undo function on the paper prototype, but we have fixed it in our figma prototype as below to improve the heuristic of User Control and Freedom.

#2 Aesthetic and Minimalist Design

In our paper prototype, we put the user profile at the top of our homepage. However, our paper prototype was in the desktop view, and our final product would be used on the mobile phone as the instruction proposed. After listing all homepage functions in a priority order, and for aesthetic design, we choose to hide the user profile into a button at the bottom because the user profile would not be as important as the diary entries.

#3 Maintaining the Secured Branding

We ought to make a public shared function on our map page for people to travel online, watching others’ video feeds in a certain location especially during the pandemic circumstances. However, it is somehow against our private and secured diary branding for Compass. Thus, we choose to eliminate this functionality.

Wireframe

As the Compass aims to assist users for self-tracking and self-reflecting, the homepage is all the diary entries listed in the chronological order. The call for action button is creating a new diary entry, where it is at the bottom where users can easily reach with their thumbs.
