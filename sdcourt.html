Usability Assessment & Redesign

Site assessed: https://www.sdcourt.ca.gov

Site redesigned: SD Court Locations

Task: Using Nielsen's 10 Heuristics to assess this site. For each of the 10 heuristics, have 2 violations with well captioned/annotated screenshots.
Duration: October 2020, 2 weeks
Team: Jinghan Cao, Michael Lyken

SD Court Locations Page analysis

Problem #1: Far distancing

As mentioned in the assessment, the list of locations is too far away from the map. And the list is aligned to the right of the website. For some browsers, users might not even see the list when they click into this site unless they scroll to the right manually. Also, users have to relook the list to check the number assigned for each court. The far distance between the map and the list makes users to take inconvenient actions wasting a lot of time.


Problem #2: Don’t know which Courthouse I should go

There is no indication about each court’s service. Users have to click into every courts’ page to figure out where they should go. The page gives some advices on choosing the court, yet detailed information is not provided.
